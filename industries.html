Sectors We Serve

Purpose-built IT
for every sector.

We work across eight primary industry verticals, with specific experience of the compliance frameworks, data protection obligations, and operational requirements each one demands.

01
Financial Services

FCA-regulated firms demand the highest standards of data security, business continuity, and audit readiness. We deliver managed IT, cyber security, and ISO 27001 programmes aligned to FCA operational resilience requirements, GDPR obligations, and MiFID II data retention rules. Our managed SOC provides continuous threat monitoring aligned to financial sector threat intelligence.

FCA ComplianceISO 27001Cyber SecurityAudit TrailsBusiness Continuity
02
Healthcare & Medical

NHS Digital standards, CQC registration requirements, and clinical data handling rules make healthcare one of the most demanding IT environments. We support GP surgeries, dental practices, private clinics, and care homes with secure infrastructure, DSPT compliance, N3/HSCN connectivity, and clinical system integration. Patient data never leaves UK-based, NHS-approved infrastructure.

DSPTHSCNCQC ReadyClinical SystemsIG Toolkit
03
Legal & Professional Services

Legal firms handle some of the most sensitive client data in existence. We implement matter-level data security, SRA compliance-aligned policies, practice management system migrations, and encrypted communication channels. Our cyber security team has specific experience with legal phishing campaigns and funds transfer fraud — a growing threat to conveyancers and commercial law firms.

SRA ComplianceMatter SecurityFTF PreventionPMS IntegrationLexcel
04
Education

Schools, colleges, and universities require secure, manageable networks that balance open learning environments with robust safeguarding and GDPR compliance. We provide BYOD policy framework, Ofsted-ready cyber security postures, Microsoft 365 Education licensing, safeguarding-compliant filtering, and full infrastructure support for single schools through to multi-academy trusts.

Ofsted ReadyMicrosoft EDUSafeguardingBYOD PolicyMAT
05
Retail & Hospitality

High-footfall environments need reliable Wi-Fi, robust PCI-DSS compliant payment infrastructure, integrated EPoS systems, and secure customer data handling. We support single-site independents through to multi-site chains with managed networking, guest Wi-Fi, CCTV, access control, and IT support that understands the operational demands of retail and food service.

PCI-DSSGuest Wi-FiEPoSCCTVMulti-Site
06
Property & Real Estate

Property firms process high volumes of personal data, handle large financial transactions, and are prime targets for conveyancing fraud. We provide GDPR-aligned data management, secure client portal infrastructure, anti-fraud email controls, and managed IT services for estate agents, property managers, and developers. AML compliance documentation and secure client onboarding workflows available.

AML SupportAnti-FraudSecure PortalsGDPRMulti-Office
07
Manufacturing & Engineering

Manufacturing environments present unique IT challenges: OT/IT convergence, SCADA system security, shift-worker device management, and distributed site connectivity. We design and manage secure industrial networks, implement endpoint protection for production environments, and provide the business continuity planning that prevents costly production shutdowns from cyber incidents.

OT/IT SecuritySCADABusiness ContinuityMulti-Site WANISO 27001
08
Government & Public Sector

Public sector organisations face stringent procurement, security classification, and data handling requirements. We hold the necessary accreditations for public sector work and have experience delivering Cyber Essentials Plus, PSN compliance, and GDPR frameworks for councils, housing associations, and government contractors. G-Cloud registered.
