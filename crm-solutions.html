Our Process

From discovery
to deployment.

We follow a structured delivery process that keeps you in control at every stage — with clear milestones, regular demonstrations, and no surprises. Most projects go from kickoff to live in 8–16 weeks.

  • 01
    Discovery & Requirements

    We spend time with your team to map your current processes, identify pain points, and define exactly what the software needs to do. The output is a detailed specification document you sign off before any development begins.

  • 02
    Design & Prototyping

    Wireframes and interactive prototypes are created so you can see and test the user interface before a line of code is written. This eliminates costly late-stage changes and ensures the design works for your team.

  • 03
    Agile Development

    Development is delivered in two-week sprints with working software demonstrated at the end of each sprint. You test real functionality throughout the build, not just at the end — keeping the project on track and on budget.

  • 04
    Testing & Quality Assurance

    Rigorous functional, performance, and security testing before any release. Your team conducts user acceptance testing (UAT) with our full support, ensuring every feature works exactly as specified in your environment.

  • 05
    Deployment, Training & Support

    Go-live is carefully managed with data migration, user training, and a hypercare period where our team is on hand to resolve any issues quickly. Ongoing support and enhancement contracts available post-launch.
