Core Infrastructure Services

Built to last.
Designed to protect.

Our engineers are fully accredited and hold manufacturer certifications for all major systems we install. Every installation comes with a comprehensive handover pack, as-built drawings, and ongoing maintenance options.

01

CCTV & Access Control

IP and HD-over-coax CCTV systems with local NVR/DVR and cloud-hosted viewing. Door access control from standalone keypads to enterprise ANPR and biometric systems. Remote management, mobile app access, and full maintenance contracts available.

02

Fire Detection & Safety

BS 5839-compliant fire detection and alarm systems. Conventional and addressable panels, L1 to M-grade systems, sprinkler integration, and emergency lighting. Designed, installed, and commissioned by BAFE-accredited engineers with annual service contracts.

03

Structured Cabling

Cat6A and fibre optic data cabling with full certification testing. Rack installation, patch panel labelling, and cable management to ANSI/TIA-568 standards. Tested to 25-year performance warranties, with full documentation for audits and moves.

04

Boardroom & AV

Fully integrated meeting and boardroom environments — display walls, video conferencing, wireless presentation systems, audio distribution, and room booking panels. Certified integrators for Microsoft Teams Rooms, Logitech, and Crestron.

Why Vertex Infrastructure

The credentials
that matter.

Physical security and infrastructure is a regulated discipline. Our engineers hold the accreditations and certifications that demonstrate competence — not just experience.

NICEIC Registered
Fully registered NICEIC contractor for all electrical and data cabling work. Competent Person Scheme membership for self-certification of electrical installations.
BAFE Accredited
BAFE SP203 accreditation for fire detection and alarm system design, installation, commissioning, and maintenance. Required by many insurers and building managers.
NSI-Approved Installer
NSI-approved for CCTV and access control installations to NCP 104 standard. Police-preferred specification for monitored systems.
Full Documentation
Every installation is accompanied by as-built drawings, test certificates, equipment datasheets, and a comprehensive handover pack — essential for insurance, audits, and future engineers.
5-Year Warranty
All installations carry a 5-year workmanship warranty backed by our own insurance. Manufacturer warranties are registered and managed on your behalf.
Maintenance Contracts
Annual service contracts with priority response SLAs. Includes all scheduled preventive maintenance, test certificates, and system health reports. 24/7 emergency call-out available.
