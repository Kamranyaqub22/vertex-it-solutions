Our Process

What happens
next.

We believe in a straightforward, pressure-free process. Here's exactly what to expect after you reach out.

  • 01
    Initial Response — Within 4 Hours

    A member of our team will acknowledge your enquiry and, if needed, ask a few clarifying questions. No automated responses — you'll hear from a real person with relevant expertise.

  • 02
    Discovery Call — 30 Minutes

    We'll schedule a short call to understand your current setup, pain points, and objectives. This is exploratory only — no obligation, no sales pressure, and no contract until you're ready.

  • 03
    Free Assessment & Proposal

    For managed IT or multi-service engagements, we'll conduct a free on-site or remote assessment of your current environment and produce a written proposal with clear pricing and a recommended scope of work.

  • 04
    Agreement & Onboarding

    Once you're ready, we'll provide a clear services agreement and onboard your team within an agreed timeframe. For managed IT clients, our onboarding team ensures a smooth, zero-disruption transition.
