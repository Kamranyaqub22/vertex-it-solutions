Company History

Our story &
where we're going.

What started as a small IT support firm serving local businesses in London has grown into one of the UK's most comprehensive technology providers — still with the same commitment to personal service and technical excellence.

  • 2010
    Founded in London
    Comprehensive IT Solutions (CITS) was established by a team of ex-corporate IT professionals who believed SMEs deserved enterprise-quality IT support without the enterprise price tag. First 20 clients onboarded in year one.
  • 2013
    Physical Security Division Launched
    Recognising growing client demand, we hired our first team of CCTV and access control engineers, enabling us to offer a genuinely end-to-end service covering both IT and physical premises security.
  • 2016
    ISO 27001 & Microsoft Gold Partnership
    We achieved ISO 27001 certification and attained Microsoft Gold Partner status — reflecting our investment in certified expertise and robust internal security practices. Both are maintained and renewed annually.
  • 2019
    Cloud & Compliance Practice Established
    A dedicated cloud migration and ISO compliance team was formed to help clients navigate digital transformation, GDPR requirements, and the move from on-premise infrastructure to Azure and Microsoft 365.
  • 2022
    National Expansion — 200+ Clients
    Our client base expanded beyond London to cover businesses across England and Wales. We now have field engineers covering all major regions, with same-day on-site response included in premium SLAs.
  • Today
    40+ Engineers. One Accountable Team.
    With over 40 certified engineers across IT, security, networking, cabling, and compliance, Comprehensive IT Solutions (CITS) delivers the breadth and depth that most IT companies can't match — and the personal service that large firms have lost.

How We Work

The principles
that guide us.

We're not just a vendor — we're a long-term partner. These values underpin every engagement, every support call, and every project we deliver.

01
Accountability First

We take full ownership of your technology estate. When something goes wrong, we own it. No finger-pointing between vendors, no grey areas of responsibility — just a single team that's accountable for everything.

02
Proactive, Not Reactive

Our monitoring tools detect issues before they become problems. We'd rather prevent a server failure at 2am than respond to a crisis at 9am. Proactive management is what separates us from traditional break-fix IT.

03
Commercial Intelligence

Good IT advice means understanding your business. We don't recommend technology for technology's sake — every solution is evaluated against your commercial objectives, budget constraints, and growth trajectory.

04
Security by Default

Every service we deliver is designed with security as a baseline, not an afterthought. From network architecture to email configuration, we enforce best practice protections without making them a burden on your team.

05
Transparent Pricing

No surprise invoices. No mid-contract price increases. Every engagement is backed by a clear scope of work, fixed monthly fees, and a plain-English contract. You always know exactly what you're paying for.

06
Always Improving

Technology evolves. So do we. Our engineers hold active certifications across Microsoft, Cisco, CompTIA, and security disciplines — and we invest continuously in training so your infrastructure is always managed at the highest standard.
