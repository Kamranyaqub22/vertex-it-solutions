Connectivity Solutions

Connectivity you
can rely on.

Downtime costs UK businesses an average of £5,600 per minute. Our connectivity solutions include built-in resilience, 24/7 monitoring, and proactive failover — so you keep working when your primary link doesn't.

Leased Lines
Uncontended synchronous connectivity from 100Mbps to 10Gbps. EFM, Ethernet over FTTC, and dark fibre options. SLA-backed with 4-hour engineer response and uptime guarantees.
Business Broadband & FTTP
SME broadband with static IP addressing, monitored SLAs, and priority support. SOGEA and FTTP to premises on all major UK exchange areas. ISDN switch-off migration planning available.
SD-WAN
Software-defined WAN for multi-site businesses. Intelligent traffic routing, application QoS, and centralised management. Combine leased line, broadband, and 4G links into one resilient WAN fabric.
4G / 5G Failover
Automatic cellular failover for critical sites. Sub-30-second switchover, dedicated SIM management, and integration with your primary router. Eliminates single-circuit dependency at modest monthly cost.
SIP Trunks & VoIP
SIP trunk provision for on-premises PBX or direct routing for Microsoft Teams Calling. Number porting, DDI management, call recording, and PCI-DSS pause-and-resume recording available.
24/7 Network Monitoring
Real-time monitoring of all circuits, routers, and switches. Proactive fault detection and automatic ticket creation. Monthly capacity reports and review calls with your named account manager.
